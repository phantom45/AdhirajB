SurviVE

An atmospheric horror game made in the Unreal Engine 4

April 2021

Itch.io Page (Game Download Link)

Github Repository

Synopsis

An "atmospheric horror" game set on an island. You find yourself stranded on a mysterious island due to a storm, and you can't seem to find your friends that were journeying with you. Do you have the courage to find out what happened to your friends?

Tech Stack

    • Game Design

    The game has a hunger system and you must use the food boxes/crates on the world. There are also batteries that you must collect if the torch on your flashlight runs out.
